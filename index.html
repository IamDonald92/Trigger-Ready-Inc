First time? Read this

Paintball 101

Do I need any experience?
None at all. Most of our players are first-timers. We brief you on the gear and the rules before you step on the field — you'll be ready in minutes.
How many people do I need?
A minimum of 4 players to book a session. There's no real maximum — bigger groups just mean bigger battles. Tell us your numbers and we'll sort the teams.
What should I wear?
Closed shoes and comfortable clothes you can move in. We provide full overalls and a chest protector over the top, so your own clothes stay protected. Long sleeves and pants are smart if you bruise easily.
Does it hurt?
A hit feels like a quick sting — gone in a second. The mask, overalls and chest protector cover the areas that matter, so it's all part of the fun.
How long is a session?
It depends on your group size and how fast you burn through your 200 paintballs. Most groups spend a couple of action-packed hours on site. Need more? Top-up paintballs are available on the day.
How do I book and pay?
Message us on WhatsApp (or use the form below) with your date and group size. A deposit secures your slot. Same-day bookings are full payment in advance.
